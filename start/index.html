Pay or appeal a parking or bus lane fine

Use this service to pay or appeal a Penalty Charge Notice (PCN) issued within Greater Manchester.

Pay a new fine Appeal

Before you start

You’ll need your Penalty Charge Notice (PCN) number and your vehicle registration number.

  • Your PCN starts with AW followed by 8 digits (for example: AW12345678).
  • Your vehicle registration (for example: RF24 JVU).
You should wait at least one full working day after your notice was issued before paying online.

If you believe your PCN was issued in error, you can make an appeal using this service.
