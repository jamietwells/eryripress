Pay a parking or bus lane fine

You can use this online service to pay a penalty charge for parking, bus lane, or moving traffic contraventions within Greater Manchester.

Please allow at least one full working day after the notice was issued before attempting to pay online.

You will need:

  • Your Penalty Charge Notice (PCN) number – it begins with AW followed by 8 digits (e.g. AW12345678)
  • Your vehicle registration (number plate)
If you believe your PCN was issued in error, you can also appeal online.
Start now

Other ways to pay

You can also pay by phone using an automated payment line. Details are provided on your PCN.

If you have any issues using this service, please contact the relevant council department directly.
